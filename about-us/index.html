PAIN 01

PAIN 02

Who we are

A digital remittance desk
for India to the world.

Money2Abroad helps Indian travel businesses manage outward remittance requests with live rates, guided documentation, online payment and confirmation tracking. The page is built for agents handling tour cost remittance, visa fees, overseas education and family-maintenance transfers.

How it works

From invoice to confirmation,
without branch runs.

The flow mirrors how travel agencies actually work: quote the customer, add the overseas beneficiary, upload the commercial document, finish digital KYC, pay online and receive confirmation when the remittance moves.

01

Register your agency desk

Create your agency profile, add branch users, and keep your onboarding documents in one secure workspace.

02

Check live remittance rates

See live currency rates before you promise a quote to the customer or commit a DMC payment.

03

Upload invoice and bank details

Add the DMC, hotel, university or visa beneficiary once, attach the invoice, and reuse verified details later.

04

Complete digital KYC and A2

Purpose code, Form A2, LRS checks and supporting documents are guided inside the same journey.

05

Lock rate and pay online

Block the chosen rate and complete payment through NEFT, RTGS or approved online collection flows.

06

Track confirmation

Receive status alerts, SWIFT references and settlement confirmations your operations team can share back with the customer.
